One-Pan Pasta – Shopping List

Must Have Items

  • 1 lb chicken & apple sausage (or other sausage)
  • 1 (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 lb rigatoni (or other pasta)
  • 3 oz Boursin cheese (garlic & herb)
  • 3 tbsp butter

Aromatics – Check Fridge

  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 3–4 cloves garlic, minced
  • Optional: parsley and/or basil for serving

Optional Items

  • Parsley and/or basil, for garnish
  • Grated Parmesan, for serving

Spices – Check Pantry First

  • 1 tsp crushed red pepper or aleppo pepper (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper

Other

  • Olive oil
  • 6–8 cups chicken stock or water
