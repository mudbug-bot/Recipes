Chicken Sausage, Braised Chicken Thigh & Red Rice – Shopping List

Must Have Items

  • 1.25 lb boneless chicken thighs
  • 1 lb andouille sausage
  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 1 can cannellini or white beans, drained and rinsed
  • 2¼ cups chicken stock (hot)

Aromatics – Check Fridge

  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Optional finish: chopped parsley
  • Optional finish: a lemon (for a squeeze of juice)

Optional Items

  • Optional finish: chopped parsley
  • Optional finish: a lemon for squeezing over at the end

Spices – Check Pantry First

  • 1 tsp paprika (smoked if possible)
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • Pinch red pepper flakes
  • Kosher salt and black pepper

Other

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
