# Saffron Couscous

**Serves:** 4 | **Prep:** 5 minutes | **Cook:** 10 minutes

## Ingredients

- 1½ cups couscous
- 1¾ cups chicken or vegetable broth
- ¼ tsp saffron threads
- 2 tbsp olive oil or butter
- ½ tsp salt
- ¼ tsp cumin (optional)
- 2 tbsp fresh parsley or mint, chopped

## Instructions

1. Warm the broth in a small saucepan. Add the saffron threads and let them steep for 5 minutes — the broth will turn a beautiful golden color.
2. Bring the saffron broth to a boil. Add olive oil (or butter) and salt.
3. Pour in the couscous, stir once, then remove from heat. Cover with a lid and let sit for 5 minutes.
4. Fluff with a fork, taste for seasoning, and stir in fresh parsley or mint before serving.

## Tips

A handful of toasted pine nuts or golden raisins stirred in at the end adds a lovely Moroccan touch.
