Shopping List

Robs New Orleans Gumbo

🛒 Must Have

  • Boneless chicken thighs – 3 to 4 lbs
  • Andouille sausage – 1 to 2 lbs
  • Okra – 2 quarts (about 2 lbs)
  • Oysters – 1 lb (with liquor, required)
  • Shrimp – 1 lb, peeled and deveined
  • Crabmeat – 1 lb (optional)
  • Chicken stock – 2 quarts (or water)
  • Canned tomatoes – 1 medium can
  • All-purpose flour – 2/3 cup
  • Bacon fat – 1/2 cup
  • Butter – 4 tbsp
  • Boiled rice (for serving)

🧄 Aromatics – Check Fridge

  • Onions – 2 medium, sliced
  • Parsley – 1 tbsp chopped

❓ Optional

  • Turkey bones/meat trimmings – 3 to 4 lbs (for homemade turkey stock)
  • Onion, celery, carrot, garlic (for turkey stock aromatics)
  • Gumbo filé
  • Lemon juice or mild vinegar (chef finish)

🌶️ Spices – Check Pantry First

  • Cayenne pepper (for chicken + table seasoning)
  • Black peppercorns (for optional turkey stock)
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Bay leaves (1 for gumbo + 2 for optional turkey stock)
  • Salt (to taste at table)

🥫 Other

  • Water (for gumbo and optional turkey stock)
