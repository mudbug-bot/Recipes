Braised Beef Chili – Shopping List

🛒 Must Have

  • 2 lb beef chuck, cut into 1½–2 inch chunks
  • 1½ cups beef stock
  • 3/4 cup strong brewed coffee
  • Optional: 1 can kidney or pinto beans, rinsed

🧄 Aromatics – Check Fridge

  • 1½ large yellow onions, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, smashed

❓ Optional

  • 3/8 tsp cinnamon (optional)
  • Optional: 1 can kidney or pinto beans, rinsed

🌶️ Spices – Check Pantry First

  • 1¼ tsp kosher salt (plus more to finish)
  • 1/2 tsp freshly cracked black pepper
  • 2 Tbsp chili powder (ancho, New Mexico, or hatch)
  • 1½ tsp ground cumin
  • 1½ tsp ground coriander
  • 1½ tsp smoked paprika
  • 3/4 tsp Mexican oregano
  • 3/8 tsp cinnamon (optional)

🥫 Other

  • 1–2 Tbsp neutral oil or beef tallow
  • 1½ Tbsp tomato paste
  • 1–1½ tsp apple cider vinegar or fresh lime juice
