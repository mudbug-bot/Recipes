Fluffy and Creamy Mashed Potatoes – Shopping List

Must Have Items

  • 3 lb russet potatoes
  • 4 oz unsalted butter
  • 1¼ cups heavy cream

Aromatics – Check Fridge

  • 1 tbsp fresh chives, chopped (for garnish)

Optional Items

  • Additional ingredients only if making one of the flavor variations (roasted garlic, herbs, cheese, etc.)

Spices – Check Pantry First

  • 2 tbsp + 2 tsp kosher salt

Other

  • None
