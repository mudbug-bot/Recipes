# Simple Cucumber Tomato Salad

**Serves:** 4 | **Prep:** 10 min

## Ingredients

- 2 medium tomatoes, diced (or a cup of halved cherry tomatoes)
- 1 English cucumber, diced
- ¼ red onion, thinly sliced
- ¼ cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved (optional)
- 2 tbsp fresh parsley or mint, chopped

### Dressing
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 1½ tbsp lemon juice (about half a lemon)
- 1 small garlic clove, minced
- ½ tsp dried oregano
- Salt & black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine the tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, olives, and fresh herbs in a bowl.
2. Whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper in a small bowl.
3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
4. Let it sit for 5–10 minutes before serving so the flavors meld.

## Tips

- Add a crumble of feta cheese on top for extra richness.
- Season just before serving so the vegetables stay crisp.
