Chocolate Mousse – Shopping List

Must Have Items

  • 6 oz (170 g) bittersweet chocolate (60–70%), chopped
  • 3 oz (85 g) unsalted butter, cut into pieces and softened
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2/3 cup (130 g) sugar
  • Optional: whipped cream and chocolate shavings, for serving

Aromatics – Check Fridge

  • None

Optional Items

  • 1 tsp vanilla extract or 1 Tbsp rum/coffee liqueur (optional)
  • Whipped cream and chocolate shavings, for serving (optional)

Spices – Check Pantry First

  • Pinch of salt

Other

  • None
