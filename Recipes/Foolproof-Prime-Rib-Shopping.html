The Foolproof Prime Rib – Shopping List

Must Have Items

  • Prime rib roast (boneless or bone-in, sized to your guests)

Aromatics – Check Fridge

  • Fresh herbs for compound butter and garnish (such as thyme or chives)
  • A head of garlic (for gravy and/or compound butter)
  • Onion, carrot, and celery (for gravy base)

Optional Items

  • Miso paste (optional umami addition to compound butter or gravy)

Spices – Check Pantry First

  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly cracked black pepper

Other

  • Neutral oil or beef fat (for initial roasting, if desired)
  • Butter (for compound butter and/or gravy)
  • Worcestershire sauce (for compound butter or gravy)
  • Soy sauce (optional for gravy)
  • Cornstarch (for thickening gravy)
  • Beef scraps or extra beef bones (for gravy, if available)
