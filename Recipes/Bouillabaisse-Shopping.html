Shopping List

Bouillabaisse – Shopping List

🛒 Must Have

  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1 lb ripe tomatoes, roughly chopped or 1 1/2 cups drained canned tomatoes or 1/2 cup tomato paste
  • 3–4 lb fish heads, bones, and trimmings or 1 quart clam juice + 1 1/2 quarts water
  • 6–8 lb assorted lean fish (and shellfish if desired)

🧄 Aromatics – Check Fridge

  • 1 cup minced onions
  • 3/4 cup minced leek (or 1/2 cup additional onions)
  • 4 cloves mashed garlic
  • 1 lb ripe tomatoes (if using fresh instead of canned/paste)
  • Parsley sprigs (6 for herbs, plus more for garnish)

❓ Optional

  • Shellfish such as clams, mussels, scallops, crab, or lobster (if desired)

🌶️ Spices – Check Pantry First

  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 tsp thyme or basil
  • 1/2 tsp fennel
  • 2 big pinches saffron (plus more to taste)
  • 2-inch piece or 1/2 tsp dried orange peel
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1 Tb salt (omit if using clam juice)

🥫 Other

  • 2 1/2 quarts water (if not fully replaced by clam juice/stock)
  • French bread for serving (to place in the soup tureen)
