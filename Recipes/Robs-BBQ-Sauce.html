Rob's BBQ Sauce

A rich, sweet-tangy barbecue sauce built with bacon fat or tallow, onion, garlic, brown sugar, cider vinegar, ketchup, and pantry spices.

Category: Spice Mixes and Sauces Stovetop Simmer 10–15 min

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp tallow or bacon fat
  • 1/3 yellow onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup light brown sugar
  • 1 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 2 cups ketchup
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp mustard powder
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 4 dashes Worcestershire sauce

Method

  1. Warm the tallow or bacon fat in a saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened.
  3. Add the garlic and cook briefly until fragrant.
  4. Stir in the brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, ketchup, paprika, mustard powder, sea salt, black pepper, and Worcestershire sauce.
  5. Once combined, simmer for 10 to 15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce is smooth and slightly thickened.
  6. Taste and adjust if needed, then use warm or cool and refrigerate.

Good With

  • Ribs
  • Pork roast or pulled pork
  • Grilled or roasted chicken
  • Burgers or meatloaf glaze
