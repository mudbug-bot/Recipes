Easy Meatloaf

Easy-Meatloaf • Serves 6 • Meat

Classic, straightforward meatloaf: ground beef, bread crumbs, and a simple ketchup-brown sugar-mustard glaze. This is the basic version that just works and makes great leftovers.

Ingredients

For the loaf

  • Ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • Milk
  • Dry bread crumbs
  • Salt and pepper

For the sauce

  • Ketchup
  • Brown sugar
  • Mustard

Method

1. Mix the loaf

  1. Heat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine the ground beef, egg, chopped onion, milk, bread crumbs, salt, and pepper.
  3. Mix just until combined, then transfer to a loaf pan and shape into a loaf.

2. Mix the sauce

  1. In a small bowl, stir together ketchup, brown sugar, and mustard.
  2. Spread the sauce evenly over the top of the meatloaf.

3. Bake

  1. Bake in the preheated oven for about 1 hour, or until the meatloaf is no longer pink in the center.
  2. For safety, an instant-read thermometer inserted in the middle should read 160°F (71°C).

Serving & Storage

Let the meatloaf rest briefly before slicing. Serve with mashed potatoes, peas, or any of your usual sides.

Leftovers: Wrap tightly and refrigerate for 3–4 days, or double-wrap and freeze for 3–4 months. Cold slices make great meatloaf sandwiches.
