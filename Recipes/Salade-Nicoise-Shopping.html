Shopping List

Salade Niçoise

🛒 Must Have

  • 8–10 small new potatoes
  • 8 oz green beans
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2–3 ripe tomatoes
  • 1 small head Boston or Bibb lettuce (or mixed greens)
  • 6–8 oz high-quality tuna in olive oil
  • 8–12 Niçoise or other small black olives
  • 6–8 anchovy fillets (optional but traditional)

🧄 Aromatics – Check Fridge

  • 1 small red onion or a few radishes (optional, thinly sliced)
  • 1 small clove garlic
  • Fresh herbs: parsley and/or basil (for garnish and vinaigrette)

❓ Optional

  • Red onion or radishes (optional)
  • Anchovy fillets (optional but traditional)
  • Fresh herbs for vinaigrette (parsley, basil, or chives)

🌶️ Spices – Check Pantry First

  • Salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper

🥫 Other

  • Dijon mustard
  • Red wine vinegar or lemon juice
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
