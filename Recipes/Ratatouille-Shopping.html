Ratatouille – Shopping List

🛒 Must Have

  • 1 lb eggplant
  • 1 lb zucchini
  • 4–5 ripe tomatoes (or 1 can good-quality tomatoes)
  • 1–2 Tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 1–2 Tbsp chopped fresh basil or thyme (or 1 tsp dried Herbes de Provence)

🧄 Aromatics – Check Fridge

  • 1 large onion
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 red or yellow bell pepper
  • 2–3 cloves garlic

❓ Optional

  • None (recipe does not explicitly list optional ingredients)

🌶️ Spices – Check Pantry First

  • Salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1–2 tsp salt for salting eggplant and zucchini

🥫 Other

  • 4 Tbsp olive oil (more as needed)
