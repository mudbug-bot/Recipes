Braised Bone-In Chicken with Umami Pan Sauce – Shopping List

🛒 Must Have

  • 4–6 bone-in chicken thighs and/or drumsticks

🧄 Aromatics – Check Fridge

  • 1 small shallot or onion, finely sliced or diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 1 tsp fresh oregano, finely chopped
  • 2 Tbsp fresh parsley, finely chopped

❓ Optional

  • None

🌶️ Spices – Check Pantry First

  • Salt
  • Freshly cracked black pepper

🥫 Other

  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 Tbsp butter
  • 1 Tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 cup chicken stock
  • 2 Tbsp cold butter, cubed (to finish the sauce)
