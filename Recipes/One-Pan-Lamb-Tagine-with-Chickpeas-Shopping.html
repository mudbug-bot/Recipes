One-Pan Lamb Tagine with Chickpeas – Shopping List

Must Have Items

  • 1kg lamb neck fillet
  • 400g can chopped tomatoes
  • 600ml chicken or lamb stock
  • 2 × 400g cans chickpeas
  • 100g dried apricots (optional)
  • 150g natural yogurt
  • Cooked couscous

Aromatics – Check Fridge

  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 4 carrots, cut into large chunks
  • 6 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • Thumb-sized piece of ginger, finely grated
  • Small bunch coriander, roughly chopped

Optional Items

  • 100g dried apricots (optional in the tagine)

Spices – Check Pantry First

  • 3 tbsp ras el hanout
  • 2 tbsp rose harissa paste (plus 1 tsp to serve)
  • ½ preserved lemon (or strip of lemon zest)
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper

Other

  • 2 tbsp olive oil (plus extra drizzle if needed)
  • Small handful flaked almonds, toasted
