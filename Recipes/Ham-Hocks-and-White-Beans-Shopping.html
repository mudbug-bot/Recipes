Shopping List

Ham Hocks & White Beans

Must Have

  • ☐ Smoked ham hocks (2–3)
  • ☐ Dried white beans, 1 lb (Great Northern, navy, or cannellini)
  • ☐ Chicken stock (8–10 cups / ~2½ qt cartons)

Aromatics – Check Fridge

  • ☐ Garlic (8 cloves)
  • ☐ Small onion (1)
  • ☐ Fresh chives (for garnish)

Optional

  • ☐ Rice vinegar (1–2 tbsp, for finishing)

Spices – Check Pantry First

  • ☐ Smoked paprika
  • ☐ Black pepper
  • ☐ Bay leaf
  • ☐ Salt (for final seasoning)

Other

  • ☐ Cornbread ingredients (highly recommended on the side)
