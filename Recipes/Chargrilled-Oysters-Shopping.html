Chargrilled Oysters – Shopping List

Must Have Items

  • Fresh oysters, on the half shell (24)
  • Unsalted butter (1 lb / 4 sticks)
  • Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Romano cheese, grated
  • Fresh French bread

Aromatics – Check Fridge

  • Garlic (8 cloves)
  • Fresh oregano (1 tbsp) — or use dried

Optional Items

  • Extra French bread (you'll want more)

Spices – Check Pantry First

  • Kosher salt (½ tsp)
  • Black pepper (1 tsp)
  • Cayenne (½ tsp)
  • Dried oregano — if not using fresh

Other

  • Charcoal or gas for grill
