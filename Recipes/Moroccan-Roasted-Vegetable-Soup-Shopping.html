Moroccan Roasted Vegetable Soup

🛒 Must Have

  • 1 red onion
  • 300g carrots
  • 300g parsnips
  • 300g peeled butternut squash
  • 1 small potato
  • 1.3l vegetable stock (or stock cubes to make this amount)
  • Greek-style yogurt (for serving, optional)

🧄 Aromatics – Check Fridge

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • Fresh mint (for garnish, if using yogurt topping)

❓ Optional

  • Greek-style yogurt and fresh mint (for serving)

🌶️ Spices – Check Pantry First

  • 1 tbsp ras el hanout
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper

🥫 Other

  • 1½ tbsp olive oil
