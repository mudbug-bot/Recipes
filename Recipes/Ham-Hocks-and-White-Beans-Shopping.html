Shopping List

Ham Hocks & White Beans

🛒 Must Have

  • Smoked ham hocks – 2 to 3
  • Dried white beans – 1 lb (Great Northern, navy, or cannellini)
  • Chicken stock – 8 to 10 cups

🧄 Aromatics – Check Fridge

  • Garlic – 8 cloves
  • Small onion – 1
  • Fresh chives – for garnish

❓ Optional

  • Rice vinegar – 1 to 2 tbsp, for finishing
  • Cornbread ingredients – strongly recommended on the side

🌶️ Spices – Check Pantry First

  • Smoked paprika
  • Black pepper
  • Bay leaf
  • Salt – for final seasoning

🥫 Other

  • Water – if the beans need more liquid during cooking
