Shopping List

Saffron Couscous

🛒 Must Have

  • Couscous – 1½ cups
  • Chicken or vegetable broth – 1¾ cups
  • Saffron threads – ¼ tsp

🧄 Aromatics – Check Fridge

  • Fresh parsley or mint – 2 tbsp, chopped

❓ Optional

  • Toasted pine nuts – small handful
  • Golden raisins – small handful

🌶️ Spices – Check Pantry First

  • Salt – ½ tsp
  • Cumin – ¼ tsp (optional)

🥫 Other

  • Olive oil or butter – 2 tbsp
Tip: Saffron is expensive — a little goes a long way. Store it in a cool, dark place to preserve potency.
