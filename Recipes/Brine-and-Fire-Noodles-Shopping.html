Brine & Fire Noodles – Shopping List

Must Have Items

  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 lb organic linguine
  • 4 to 6 oil-packed anchovy fillets
  • 1/2 cup pitted black olives, chopped
  • 1/2 cup chopped Kalamata olives
  • 1/4 cup capers, rinsed and chopped
  • 1 large 28 oz can whole peeled tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons sherry vinegar
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup freshly grated Parmigiano Reggiano

Aromatics – Check Fridge

  • 8 to 10 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup chopped flat-leaf parsley
  • Optional handful of green olives

Optional Items

  • Optional handful of green olives

Spices – Check Pantry First

  • 2 teaspoons ancho chili powder or red pepper flakes
  • Pinch dried oregano
  • Fresh cracked black pepper

Other

  • None
