Rob's Cajun Spice Mix

Robs-Cajun-Spice-Mix • Spice Blend

A house Cajun blend with enough paprika and garlic to build body, plus cayenne for heat. Use it as a dry rub, all-purpose seasoning, or a backbone for Cajun-inspired soups and stews.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 tbsp paprika
  • 2 tbsp garlic powder
  • 2 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 tbsp oregano
  • 1 tbsp onion powder
  • 1 tbsp thyme
  • 1 tbsp cumin (optional)

Method

  1. Add all ingredients to a small bowl.
  2. Whisk or stir until the spices are evenly combined.
  3. Transfer to an airtight jar or spice container.
  4. Store in a cool, dark place for up to several months.
Usage: Rub onto chicken, pork, shrimp, or fish before grilling or roasting; sprinkle over potatoes or vegetables; or stir into soups, stews, and gumbos. For less heat, cut the cayenne back to 1–2 teaspoons.
