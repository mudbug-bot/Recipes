## Why Make Your Own?

Because fresh tortillas:

- Taste better
- Stretch better
- Toast better
- And don't need preservatives to stay "soft" for three weeks

These are perfect for tacos, wraps, quesadillas, breakfast burritos, or just tearing apart and eating straight off the pan (highly encouraged).

## Ingredients

- 1½ cups all-purpose flour
- ½ teaspoon baking powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ cup warm water
- 3 tablespoons + 1 teaspoon olive oil

## Instructions

- Mix flour, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
- Add warm water and olive oil.
- Knead for a few minutes until smooth and elastic.
- Divide into 8 equal pieces and roll into balls.
- Let rest for 15 minutes (this makes rolling easier).
- Roll each ball out thin.
- Cook on a hot pan over high heat, about 45 seconds per side. No oil needed.

They should puff slightly and get light brown spots. That's the goal.

## Tips

- If they shrink back while rolling, let them rest another 5 minutes.
- Cast iron works great, but any heavy pan will do.
- Stack them in a towel as they finish to keep warm and flexible.
