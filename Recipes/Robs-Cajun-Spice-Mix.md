# Rob's Cajun Spice Mix

## Ingredients
- 2 1/2 tbsp paprika
- 2 tbsp garlic powder
- 2 tbsp salt
- 1 tbsp black pepper
- 1 tbsp cayenne pepper
- 1 tbsp oregano
- 1 tbsp onion powder
- 1 tbsp thyme
- 1 tbsp cumin (optional)

## Instructions
1. Add all ingredients to a small bowl.
2. Whisk or stir with a spoon until the spices are evenly combined.
3. Transfer to an airtight jar or spice container.
4. Store in a cool, dark place for up to several months.

## Usage Notes
- Use as a dry rub for chicken, pork, shrimp, or fish.
- Sprinkle over roasted potatoes or vegetables.
- Stir into soups, stews, and gumbos for Cajun flavor.
- If you prefer less heat, reduce the cayenne to 1–2 teaspoons.
