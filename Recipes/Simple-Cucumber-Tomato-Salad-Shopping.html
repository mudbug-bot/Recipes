Simple Cucumber Tomato Salad – Shopping List

Must Have Items

  • Tomatoes (2 medium, or 1 cup cherry tomatoes)
  • English cucumber (1)
  • Lemon (1)
  • Fresh parsley or mint

Aromatics – Check Fridge

  • Red onion (¼)
  • Garlic (1 small clove)

Optional Items

  • Kalamata olives (¼ cup, pitted)
  • Feta cheese (for topping)

Spices – Check Pantry First

  • Dried oregano (½ tsp)
  • Salt & black pepper

Other

  • Olive oil (3 tbsp)
