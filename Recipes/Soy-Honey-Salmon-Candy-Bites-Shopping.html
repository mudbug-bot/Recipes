Soy-Honey Salmon Candy Bites – Shopping List

Must Have Items

  • 1–2 lb boneless, skinless salmon fillet
  • 2 tbsp tamari
  • 2–3 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce

Aromatics – Check Fridge

  • Fresh chives, thinly sliced
  • Lime or lemon (for zest)

Optional Items

  • None (recipe does not specify optional ingredients)

Spices – Check Pantry First

  • Kosher salt

Other

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Parchment paper (for lining sheet pan)
