Shopping List

Perfect Prime Rib Guide

🛒 Must Have

  • Prime rib roast (bone-in preferred; size based on 1 lb per person for bone-in or 8 oz per person boneless)

🧄 Aromatics – Check Fridge

  • Fresh thyme (for finishing rub)
  • Fresh oregano (for finishing rub)
  • Fresh chives (for finishing rub)
  • Garlic (for roasted garlic paste in the finishing rub)

❓ Optional

  • Miso paste (optional, for finishing rub)
  • Additional herbs or aromatics if you prefer a different finishing profile

🌶️ Spices – Check Pantry First

  • Kosher salt (for dry brining)
  • Freshly cracked black pepper (for finishing, not brining)
  • Ancho chili powder (optional, for finishing rub)

🥫 Other

  • Butter or ghee (for finishing rub and basting)
  • Neutral oil or beef fat (optional, for initial roasting)
  • Beef scraps or extra beef bones (optional, for gravy)
  • Onion, carrot, and celery (for gravy base)
  • Cornstarch (for gravy thickening, optional)
  • Worcestershire sauce (optional, for gravy)
  • Soy sauce (optional, for gravy)
