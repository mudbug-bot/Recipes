Mushroom Risotto

🛒 Must Have

  • Unsalted butter – 5 Tbl
  • Arborio rice – 2 cups
  • Chicken stock – 4 to 6 cups
  • Wild mushrooms – 3/4 lb
  • Heavy cream – 1 cup
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese – 1/2 cup

🧄 Aromatics – Check Fridge

  • Shallots – enough for 3 Tbl minced
  • Fresh parsley – 2 Tbl chopped
  • Fresh thyme – 1 tsp chopped

❓ Optional

  • Mix of mushrooms such as shiitakes, porcinis, morels, chanterelles, creminis, portobellos, and oysters

🌶️ Spices – Check Pantry First

  • Kosher salt – 1 Tbl
  • Freshly ground black pepper – 1 tsp

🥫 Other

  • No additional items
