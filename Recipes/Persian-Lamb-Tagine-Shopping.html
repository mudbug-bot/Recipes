Shopping List

Persian Lamb Tagine

🛒 Must Have

  • 2kg lamb neck fillets
  • 300g ready-to-eat dried apricots
  • 250g ready-to-eat dried pitted dates
  • 100g shelled pistachios
  • Cooked couscous or basmati rice (for serving)

🧄 Aromatics – Check Fridge

  • 3 medium onions
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • Small bunch coriander leaves, roughly chopped

❓ Optional

  • None (recipe does not mark optional ingredients)

🌶️ Spices – Check Pantry First

  • 4 tsp ground cumin
  • 4 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp hot chilli powder
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • Large pinch of saffron
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper

🥫 Other

  • 5 tbsp mild olive oil or sunflower oil
  • 25g cornflour
  • 2 tsp rosewater
  • Water (1.5 litres) for braising
