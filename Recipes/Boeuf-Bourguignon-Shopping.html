Boeuf Bourguignon – Shopping List

Must Have Items

  • A 6-ounce chunk of bacon
  • 3 lbs lean stewing beef cut into 2-inch cubes
  • 3 cups full-bodied young red wine
  • 2 to 3 cups brown beef stock or canned beef bouillon
  • 18 to 24 small white onions (for brown-braised onions)
  • 1 lb fresh mushrooms (plus 1/2 lb for sautéed mushrooms, total 1 1/2 lb)

Aromatics – Check Fridge

  • 1 sliced carrot
  • 1 sliced onion
  • 2 cloves mashed garlic
  • Parsley sprigs (for stew and herb bouquet)
  • Optional: 1 to 2 Tb minced shallots or green onions (for sautéed mushrooms)

Optional Items

  • 1 to 2 Tb minced shallots or green onions (only if you want them in the mushroom garnish)

Spices – Check Pantry First

  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 tsp thyme (for stew and herb bouquet)
  • 2 bay leaves total (1 crumbled for stew; 1/2 leaf in herb bouquet)

Other

  • 1 Tb olive oil or cooking oil
  • 2 Tb flour (plus more as needed for browning/roux)
  • 1 1/2 Tb butter (for braised onions)
  • 1 1/2 Tb oil (for braised onions)
  • 1/2 cup brown stock, canned beef bouillon, dry white wine, red wine, or water (for braised onions)
  • 2 Tb butter (for sautéed mushrooms)
  • 1 Tb oil (for sautéed mushrooms)
