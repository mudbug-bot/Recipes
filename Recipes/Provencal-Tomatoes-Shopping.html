Shopping List

Provençal Tomatoes

🛒 Must Have

  • 6 medium ripe tomatoes
  • Fresh breadcrumbs (1/3–1/2 cup)
  • 2–3 Tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 1–2 Tbsp chopped fresh herbs (thyme, oregano, or basil) or dried Herbes de Provence

🧄 Aromatics – Check Fridge

  • 1–2 cloves garlic

❓ Optional

  • Parmesan or Gruyère cheese (for variation in the breadcrumb topping)

🌶️ Spices – Check Pantry First

  • Salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper

🥫 Other

  • 3–4 Tbsp olive oil (plus extra for drizzling)
