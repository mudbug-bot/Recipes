Shopping List

Red Beans & Rice

🛒 Must Have

  • Dried red beans – 2 pounds
  • Smoked ham hocks – 2
  • Smoked sausage – 1 pound
  • Salt pork – 2 small pieces
  • Cooked white rice – for serving

🧄 Aromatics – Check Fridge

  • White or yellow onion – 1
  • Green pepper – 1
  • Celery – 2 stalks
  • Parsley – 1/2 cup chopped
  • Garlic – 10 cloves

❓ Optional

  • Abita Amber beer – 1 bottle
  • Cinnamon stick – 1
  • Chicken stock – for thinning if needed
  • Fried chicken or pork chops – for serving alongside

🌶️ Spices – Check Pantry First

  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Dried oregano
  • Dried thyme
  • Bay leaves
  • Black pepper
  • White pepper
  • Cayenne pepper

🥫 Other

  • Water – 1 gallon to start, plus more if needed
