Fast, Fresh Chicken Noodle Soup – Shopping List

🛒 Must Have

  • 1 whole roasted chicken (for meat and bones)
  • Egg noodles (6–8 oz)
  • Optional: high-quality low-sodium organic or free-range broth (if not using only water)

🧄 Aromatics – Check Fridge

  • Onions (for 1 large onion, plus skins/ends for stock)
  • 3 carrots
  • 2 celery stalks
  • 4 cloves garlic (for stock)
  • 5 cloves garlic (for soup)
  • 1–2 inches fresh ginger

❓ Optional

  • Hot sauce, for serving (optional)

🌶️ Spices – Check Pantry First

  • 1½–2 tsp whole black peppercorns
  • Kosher salt, to taste
  • Fresh cracked black pepper, to finish

🥫 Other

  • 8–10 cups water (or water + prepared broth)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil or reserved chicken fat
  • ½–1 tsp soy sauce
  • ½ tsp Worcestershire sauce
