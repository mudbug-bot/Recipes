Shopping List

Ultimate Braised Beef Short Ribs

🛒 Must Have

  • 5–6 lbs bone-in beef short ribs (about 12 ribs)
  • 1 (750-ml) bottle full-bodied red wine
  • 4–6 cups veal stock or beef broth

🧄 Aromatics – Check Fridge

  • 1 large yellow onion
  • 2 large carrots
  • 2 ribs celery
  • 5 large garlic cloves
  • Fresh herbs: 5 sprigs thyme, 2 sprigs rosemary
  • Minced fresh herbs (chives or parsley), to finish

❓ Optional

  • None

🌶️ Spices – Check Pantry First

  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 bay leaves

🥫 Other

  • 1 tbsp olive oil (plus more if needed)
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • ⅓ cup tomato purée
