Leek and Potato Soup, Two Ways

🛒 Must Have

  • 6–8 cups water or light chicken stock
  • 2–4 Tbsp cream or butter (for finishing, optional)

🧄 Aromatics – Check Fridge

  • 3 large leeks (white and light green parts only)
  • 3 medium potatoes
  • Fresh chives or parsley (for garnish)

❓ Optional

  • Extra cream (½–1 cup) if making the puréed creamy version

🌶️ Spices – Check Pantry First

  • 2–3 tsp salt (to taste)
  • Freshly ground black pepper

🥫 Other

  • None
