Shopping List

BBQ Ribs

🛒 Must Have

  • 3 full racks pork spareribs, 3–4 lbs each
  • White vinegar – 2 cups
  • Paprika – 1/2 cup
  • Garlic powder – 1/4 cup
  • Brown sugar – 1/4 cup
  • Sugar – 1/4 cup
  • BBQ sauce – 1 recipe or enough for serving on the side

🧄 Aromatics – Check Fridge

  • No fresh aromatics required for the rib recipe itself

❓ Optional

  • Extra ribs if cooking for a crowd
  • Beef ribs if using the same dry rub on another cut

🌶️ Spices – Check Pantry First

  • Onion powder – 2 Tbl
  • Freshly ground black pepper – 1 Tbl
  • Kosher salt – 2 Tbl
  • Creole seasoning – 1 Tbl

🥫 Other

  • Paper towels
  • Fuel for grill / smoker setup
