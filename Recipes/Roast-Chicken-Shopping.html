Roast Chicken – Shopping List

Must Have Items

  • 1 whole chicken (3½–4½ lb / 1.6–2 kg)

Aromatics – Check Fridge

  • 1 small onion
  • 1 small carrot
  • 1 small celery stalk
  • 2–3 garlic cloves (optional)
  • 1–2 sprigs fresh thyme or tarragon (optional)

Optional Items

  • Fresh herbs (thyme or tarragon) – optional but nice

Spices – Check Pantry First

  • Salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Other

  • 2–3 Tbsp softened butter
  • 1–2 Tbsp olive oil or additional butter (for basting)
  • 1/2–1 cup chicken stock or water (for pan sauce)
