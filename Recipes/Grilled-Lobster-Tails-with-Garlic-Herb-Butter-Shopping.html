Grilled Lobster Tails with Garlic Herb Butter

🛒 Must Have

  • 2 lobster tails (6–8 oz each)

🧄 Aromatics – Check Fridge

  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tsp lemon zest
  • Lemon wedges (for serving)

❓ Optional

  • Pinch of cayenne pepper (optional, for heat in the butter)

🌶️ Spices – Check Pantry First

  • Salt and pepper
  • Pinch of smoked paprika

🥫 Other

  • Olive oil
  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter, melted
