# Skewered Scallops over Mushroom Risotto

Don’t be intimidated. Risotto is easy. Just take your time.

## Yield
6 servings

## Ingredients
- 2 1/2–3 lbs large sea scallops
- 6 long metal skewers
- 1/2 cup No-stick Grilling Marinade for Seafood (page 208 in source book)
- 2 tsp kosher salt
- 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
- 1 recipe Mushroom Risotto

## Instructions
1. Divide the scallops evenly onto the 6 skewers.
2. Brush the scallops with the marinade and allow them to marinate at room temperature for 15 minutes.
3. Sprinkle the scallops with the salt and pepper.
4. Cook over direct high heat until the scallops are opaque in the center, 5–7 minutes, turning the scallops once while cooking.
5. Place the risotto on serving dishes and remove the scallops from the skewers.
6. Place the scallops atop the Mushroom Risotto and serve.
