Rich and Creamy Macaroni and Cheese – Shopping List

Must Have Items

  • 1½ quart heavy cream
  • 3 cups freshly shredded mozzarella
  • 2 cups freshly shredded sharp cheddar
  • 2 cups freshly shredded parmesan
  • 1 block Boursin cheese
  • 1 lb elbow macaroni
  • 3/4 lb bacon

Aromatics – Check Fridge

  • 10 thyme sprigs
  • 8 cloves garlic
  • 3 tablespoons chopped chives

Optional Items

  • None (recipe does not specify optional ingredients)

Spices – Check Pantry First

  • 10 whole black peppercorns
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Other

  • 3/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1 cup water
