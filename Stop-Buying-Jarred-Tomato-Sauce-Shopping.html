Homemade Tomato Sauce – Shopping List

Must Have Items

  • 1 large can San Marzano–style whole tomatoes (or any whole peeled tomatoes)

Aromatics – Check Fridge

  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1–2 tablespoons fresh basil

Optional Items

  • Red pepper flakes or Aleppo pepper
  • Butter (2 tbsp, optional finish)
  • Anchovy (optional add-in)
  • Extra chili flake
  • Onion (if you want to add it as a variation)
  • Parmesan rind
  • Ground meat (for a quick ragù variation)

Spices – Check Pantry First

  • Sea salt
  • Red pepper flakes or Aleppo pepper (if using)

Other

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
