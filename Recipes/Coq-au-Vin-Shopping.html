Coq au Vin – Shopping List

Must Have Items

  • 1 (3–4 lb) chicken cut into serving pieces (or 8 bone-in thighs/drumsticks)
  • 4–6 oz slab bacon or thick-cut bacon, cut into lardons
  • 2–3 cups red wine
  • 1–2 cups chicken stock
  • 8 oz mushrooms, quartered
  • 18–24 small pearl onions, peeled

Aromatics – Check Fridge

  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 2 Tbsp chopped fresh parsley (for garnish)

Optional Items

  • None

Spices – Check Pantry First

  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1–2 Tbsp flour
  • 1 Tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 tsp dried thyme

Other

  • 2–3 Tbsp butter or oil (as needed for browning)
  • 2 Tbsp butter (for garnish onions and mushrooms)
  • 1 Tbsp oil (for garnish onions and mushrooms)
