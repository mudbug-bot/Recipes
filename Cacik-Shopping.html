Cacik – Shopping List

Must Have Items

  • 1 cucumber
  • 18 oz (about 2¼ cups) whole-milk yogurt
  • Leaves from 1 bunch fresh mint, chopped

Aromatics – Check Fridge

  • 1–2 cloves garlic

Optional Items

  • None

Spices – Check Pantry First

  • 1 tsp dried mint
  • 1–2 tsp salt

Other

  • Extra-virgin olive oil, to drizzle
